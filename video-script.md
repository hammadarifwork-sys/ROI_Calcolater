# AXOVION — LinkedIn & YouTube Video Script

## Video Title: "I Built an AI Employee That Replaced My Sales Team"

**Duration:** 60-90 seconds
**Format:** Vertical (9:16) for LinkedIn + Horizontal (16:9) for YouTube
**Tone:** Direct, honest, no fluff — matches Hammad's style

---

## SCENE 1: HOOK (0-5 sec)

**Visual:** Black screen. Text appears with typing effect.

**Text on screen:**
"I fired my sales team."

**Voiceover (Hammad):**
"I fired my sales team last month. Not because they were bad. Because I found something better."

---

## SCENE 2: THE PROBLEM (5-20 sec)

**Visual:** Split screen. Left side: person drowning in phone notifications. Right side: empty chair with "Hiring..." sign.

**Text overlays:**
- "200+ DMs a day"
- "Missed leads = lost revenue"
- "Hiring costs $4,000+/mo"

**Voiceover:**
"If you run an e-commerce brand, you know the pain. Hundreds of DMs. Missed follow-ups. Customers waiting hours for a reply. And hiring? Good luck finding someone who works 24/7 without complaining."

---

## SCENE 3: THE SOLUTION (20-40 sec)

**Visual:** Screen recording of AXOVION AI chatbot in action. Real conversation flow. Then voice agent waveform. Then CRM dashboard.

**Text overlays:**
- "AI Chatbot — All Platforms"
- "AI Voice Agent — Inbound & Outbound"
- "AI Employee — Any Workflow"
- "Built-in CRM — Coming Soon"

**Voiceover:**
"So I built AXOVION. An AI chatbot that handles every DM on Instagram, WhatsApp, Facebook — instantly. An AI voice agent that makes and takes calls, qualifies leads, books appointments. And AI employees that do whatever repetitive task is eating your time. All connected to a CRM that tracks every conversation."

---

## SCENE 4: PROOF (40-55 sec)

**Visual:** Simple stat cards animating in.

**Text overlays:**
- "10,000+ conversations handled"
- "3x faster response time"
- "85% cost reduction"
- "24/7 — never sleeps, never calls in sick"

**Voiceover:**
"In 30 days, our AI handled over ten thousand conversations. Response time went from hours to seconds. And our support costs? Down 85 percent. The best part? It never sleeps, never calls in sick, and never asks for a raise."

---

## SCENE 5: CTA (55-70 sec)

**Visual:** Your face (Hammad) looking directly at camera. Calm, confident.

**Text overlay:**
"Book a free 15-min demo. No pitch deck. Just proof."

**Voiceover:**
"Look, I'm not here to sell you a dream. I'm here to show you exactly how this works for your business. Book a free 15-minute demo. No pitch deck, no slides — just a live walkthrough of your exact use case. Link in comments."

**Final screen:**
- AXOVION logo
- "AI Automation That Works While You Sleep"
- CTA button: "Book Free Demo"

## MUSIC

**Style:** Upbeat tech corporate, minimal
**BPM:** 120-128
**Mood:** Confident, modern, not overly hype
**Volume:** -20dB under voiceover

---

## BRAND COLORS FOR VIDEO

- Background: #020203 (deep black)
- Text: #EDEDEF (off-white)
- Accent: #5E6AD2 (indigo)
- Success: #22C55E (green for stats)
- Muted: #8A8F98 (for secondary text)

## FONTS

- Headlines: Space Grotesk Bold
- Body: DM Sans Regular/Medium

---

## SHOOTING NOTES

- Film in good lighting (ring light or natural window light)
- Use a clean background (solid wall or blurred office)
- Speak directly to camera for CTA scene
- Keep energy calm but confident — not hype-y
- Record voiceover separately in a quiet room for best quality
